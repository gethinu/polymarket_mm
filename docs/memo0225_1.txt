If you are copying trades without checking these metrics, you are flying blind. Here is how to open your eyes.
1. The "Time Profitable" Metric: The Truth Serum
Most traders only care about the final result: "Did they make money? "Real analysts ask: "How much pain did they endure to get there?"
Look at this section from the tool's output:
plaintext

What this tells you: This trader was underwater for 66% of the trade. They spent twice as much time losing as they did winning.
The Lesson: If you copy-traded this wallet, you would have been staring at red numbers for most of the hour. You need "diamond hands" to follow them.
The Alpha: Look for traders with >80% Time Profitable. These are the "Snipers." Their entry timing is so perfect that they go green immediately and stay green. That is who you want to copy.
2. The Hedge Check: Finding Free Money
This is the most critical metric in the entire script. It tells you if the trader is gambling or printing cash.
plaintext

The Math:In a binary market, YES + NO always settles at $1.00.

If the Combined Avg is $0.95, they have locked in a 5% risk-free profit. It doesn't matter who wins; they paid 95 cents for a dollar bill.
The Strategy:Run this script on the top 10 holders of a market. If you find someone with a Combined Avg < $1.00, stop everything. Analyze when they bought. You just found an arbitrage opportunity.
